❓ FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about owning your Whop tools instead of renting them.

Do I need technical skills to use these tools?

+
No technical skills required. Phantom Frames work directly in Google Sheets—if you can use a spreadsheet, you can use our free tools. Psycho Modules and Daemon Infrastructure include one-click installers with step-by-step video guides. Sovereign Infrastructure includes white-glove setup support where we help you configure everything.

How is my data protected with local tools?

+
All our tools run 100% on your local machine. We don't collect telemetry, usage data, or any business information. There are no servers to breach because your data never leaves your computer. You have complete sovereignty over your business intelligence.

What operating systems are supported?

+
Our Python-based tools (Psycho Modules, Daemon Infrastructure, Sovereign Infrastructure) work on Windows 10/11 and major Linux distributions including Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora, and Arch. Phantom Frames work anywhere Google Sheets runs—Windows, Mac, Linux, and even mobile browsers.

What's included with "lifetime updates"?

+
Every purchase includes free updates forever. As Whop adds new features and we enhance our tools, you'll receive all improvements at no additional cost. Updates include bug fixes, performance improvements, new features, and compatibility updates for new operating system versions.

Can I get a refund if it doesn't work for me?

+
Absolutely. All paid tools include a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, email us within 30 days of purchase and we'll refund 100% of your payment—no questions asked. We want you to buy with complete confidence.

How does the local AI work without internet?

+
Our Daemon and Sovereign Infrastructure tools use Ollama to run AI models directly on your computer. During initial setup, you download the AI model once (about 4GB), then it runs entirely locally. No internet required for AI analysis after setup. Your prompts and business data never touch external servers.

Do you offer bundle pricing for multiple tools?

+
Yes! Purchase any two paid tools and get 15% off automatically. Purchase all three paid tools together and save 25% ($320 instead of $427). Bundle discounts are applied automatically at checkout. The free Revenue Calculator is always included with any purchase.

Can I use these tools for multiple Whop accounts?

+
Yes. Your purchase license allows unlimited personal use across all your Whop accounts and businesses. Whether you run one Whop or ten, you're covered with a single purchase. The only restriction is sharing the tools with others who haven't purchased.

What kind of support do you provide?

+
All purchases include email support with 24-48 hour response times. Sovereign Infrastructure ($299) includes priority support with same-day responses and white-glove setup assistance via screen share. We also maintain comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and a community Discord for peer support.

How do these tools integrate with Whop?

+
Our tools work with data you export from Whop or enter manually. For analytics tools, you can import your Whop transaction data via CSV exports. WhopClipper works with any video content you create for your Whop. We designed everything to complement Whop's native features without requiring API access that could compromise your account security.

What makes this different from other creator tools?

+
Three key differences: (1) Ownership - you buy once and own forever, no recurring fees ever. (2) Privacy - everything runs locally, your data never touches our servers or any third party. (3) Whop-specific - these tools are built exclusively for Whop creators, not generic SaaS adapted for creators. Every feature addresses real Whop growth challenges.
